Zero-storage reporting means the product does not become a second long-lived copy of tenant data. Instead, the browser collects the snapshot after sign-in, keeps it in active memory, and lets the user export only what they actually need.

## Operational value

This model reduces storage exposure while still giving teams a modern reporting interface and downloadable artifacts.

## What remains under operator control

Downloaded files still need to be handled according to the organization's own retention and governance policies, but the application itself does not retain a tenant warehouse behind the scenes.
